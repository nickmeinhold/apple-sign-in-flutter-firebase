Go to [Certificates, Identifiers & Profiles - Apple Developer > Auth Keys](https://developer.apple.com/account/resources/authkeys/list) 

Click the + button 

<kbd><img width="400" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/1059276/84153936-d9e3c780-aaa9-11ea-84cd-0e2d643e21ab.png"></kbd>

Enable Sign In With Apple and click Configure 

<kbd><img width="400" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/1059276/84153941-dbad8b00-aaa9-11ea-96b5-1f14fccd3adc.png"></kbd>

<kbd><img width="400" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/1059276/84153946-dcdeb800-aaa9-11ea-9fda-725c8bffa322.png"></kbd>

<kbd><img width="400" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/1059276/84154033-e536f300-aaa9-11ea-9156-b1ff22d83f22.png"></kbd>
