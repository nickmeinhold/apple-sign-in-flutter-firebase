Go to [Certificates, Identifiers & Profiles - Apple Developer > App Ids](https://developer.apple.com/account/resources/identifiers/list/bundleId)

Click the + button 
<img width="1233" alt="Screen Shot 2020-06-09 at 9 37 50 pm" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/1059276/84146575-3f7e8680-aa9f-11ea-8b46-62c4d9df98d2.png">

Make sure App Ids is selected and hit Continue 

<img width="1167" alt="Screen Shot 2020-06-09 at 9 39 15 pm" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/1059276/84146582-41484a00-aa9f-11ea-9dc5-f801f248cdf6.png">

Enter a description and Identifier and hit Continue 
- NOTE: the Identifier must be the bundle id from the Flutter app
